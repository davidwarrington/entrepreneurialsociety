What we do

We offer unique opportunities for all society members, including meeting regular guest speakers from various industries. This includes opportunites to interact and engage through hands on tasks and collaborate with other like minded individuals.

Our regular events are designed to enhance student's transferable skills and gain valuable experiences which can be used for freelancing opportunities, venture start up development or within a graduate scheme.

Our dedicated committee members design and customise events, which are tailored to suit the needs and interests of society members.

Networking

The Entrepreneurial Society encourages networking opportunities with other students, members of staff and industry professionals.

Guest Speakers

We create opportunities for you to learn and network with industry specific guest speakers. It is a great chance for you to learn from the professionals.

Start-Ups

Do you have an idea or Start-Up concept that needs help with development? The Entrepreneurial Society can assist you with this.

Consultancy

Want to start your own business and management consultancy? Come along to our society events and we can sign post you in the right direction.

Enhance Employability

Our ongoing hands on activities can help you pick up extra skills for your CV, with an opportunity to ask our guest speakers questions.

Freelancing

Want to kick start your career through freelancing? We can sign post you to the right people, who can help you build your venture.

Self-Employment

Interested in working for yourself and want to learn how? Come and join us in our regular practical activities, which are supported by guest speakers from various industries.

Personal Branding

Want to learn how to build your brand? Our regular events are supported by guest speakers from various industries, which gives you an opportunity to learn and practice in building your personal brand.

Social Media

Need social media advice? Find which social media channel is best for you. We have access to social media specialists who can help you understand what you need.
